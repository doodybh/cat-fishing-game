How to Play

Points

  • Catch fish to earn points
  • The bigger the fish, the higher the points.

Avoid

  • The stinky boots (-1 life)
  • Any kinds of trash (-1 life)
  • If it dont look edible dont catch it.

Goal

Score as high as you can before you run out of lives.

Note from Whisky: catch the good fish… my family is counting on you.
